SPEE

This shiny app gather key results of the SPEE aerial survey which occured during each season of 2019 to 2020 and winter 2021 (more informaton here).

Among this results :

  • Raw sightings of megafauna
  • Observation rate
  • DSM prediction densities
  • Relative index of densities which correspond to the mean percentage of occupation of each cell of the grid by species
